ERP Launches New Website

Dave Tiberi, President of Emergency Response Protocol LLP., announced today the launch of the company's updated website: Erpsafety.com. Emergency Response Protocol is a state-of-art web-based logistical and situational awareness tool designed to provide instant access to first responders and facility staff in the event of nearly any conceivable emergency. "Erpsafety.com is not only designed to provide prospective clients a comprehensive overview on the features and benefits of the ERP Product, but we also want to make it a free on-line resource for everyone where key information on emergency response best practices can be shared," Tiberi said.
