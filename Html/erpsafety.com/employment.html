Employment

ERP is looking for talented program developers and photographers for an exciting and ongoing expansion of our staff. If you are driven by creativity and innovation then we would love to speak with you! We are an Equal Opportunity Employer E/O/E.

Program Developers

The following competencies are preferred, knowledge of public safety and facilities management, government contracting experience,project management and programming for content sensitive information would be a plus.

The successful candidate will be working on an innovative portal with the following technologies being part of your talents: ASP.NET, HTML, jquery , JavaScript, Windows Server management, photography, and image editing to name a few. We will be working on projects with strict deadlines and sensitive information, so you need to have good organizational and time management skills as well as being adept at creative thinking. A background check will be required.

In exchange for these talent we offer a friendly and fun work environment,with an opportunity to be on the front end of some of the newest cutting edge technology available in its field. Interested applicants are asked to send their resume via e-mail to Employment@erpsafety.com

Photographers

The following competencies are preferred, ability to read a blueprint, photo capturing experience, photo processing experience, and the ability to adjust to changing priorities . Having your own photography equipment is a plus.

The successful candidate(s) will be working with the current photography team on several different contracts. We will be working on projects with strict deadlines and sensitive information, so good organizational and time management skills are essential, as well as being adept at creative thinking. A background check will be required.

In exchange for these talents we offer a friendly and fun work environment, with an opportunity to be on the front end of some of the newest cutting edge technology available in its field. Interested applicants are asked to send their resume via e-mail to Employment@erpsafety.com
