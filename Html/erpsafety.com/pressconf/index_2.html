Governor's Press Conference on School Safety Plans

Emergency Response Protocol Home Governor's News Release

Chief Ken McLaughlin's Comments at Press Conference:"In response to the numerous incidents of violence that have occurred in schools across the nation, the Ocean View Police Department has, over the past ten years, enhanced relationships with area first-responders and school officials. Officers have received advanced training to prepare them to respond to critical incidents and a state-of-the-art software application was developed in conjunction with a Newport, DE based company, Emergency Response Protocol. The software contains building blueprints and site plans, as well as detailed photographs of the school and other critical information."
