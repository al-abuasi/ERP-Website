About Us


Our Story

Emergency Response Protocol (ERP) was founded in 2007 by Angela Tiberi and is a qualified Women Owned Business.

For over 20 years Angela had worked alongside her husband Dave Tiberi as co-owner of TNT Video, Multimedia and Television Productions.

During this time, Angela served as director and producer of numerous safety and training videos as well as instructional CD-ROMS and web-based tutorials.


Through this experience Angela began to appreciate the need for a dynamic interactive tool for first-responders which would provide them vital situational awareness in the field.

This led to the development and release of ERP's landmark Emergency Management software application now in use at schools, universities and other facilities throughout the area.

ERP incorporates the latest technology and the best practices in the industry, from conception and design to comprehensive ongoing maintenance and modernization.


What We Do

ERP is focused on bringing safety and security to all areas of business, public or private.

We do this with uncompromising integrity, character, and trust for all of our customers, employees and the communities that we live and operate in.

Our passion to provide the safest operating environments for schools, businesses, and public domains drive us to continuously improve the level of service and protection we provide.
